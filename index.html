Frequently asked questions

  • Where's my cookie warning?
    This site doesn't use cookies. Wow!
  • How fresh is this data?
    The data lags behind the sources by at least an hour.
  • How accurate is this data?
    Click on a meeting to check by comparing it the original source.
  • Can I really go to all these meetings?
    No. Some meetings have specific intended audiences, which are shown in the list. Some meetings have other requirements in the meeting notes, you will see this when you click through to the SMART Recovery meeting page.
  • What about meetings in other languages?
    This is not an exhaustive list of all SMART Recovery meetings globally. There are meetings close to 24 hours a day in English, so it is possible to "follow the sun" and attend meetings at any time, but this is not true for other languages.
  • Are there more English-language meetings?
    Yes, there are. See https://www.smartrecoveryinternational.org/meetings for a list of them.
